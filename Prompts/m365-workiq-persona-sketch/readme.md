## Overview

This visualization presents a one-page representation of my professional work life, leadership approach, key responsibilities, stakeholder ecosystem, values, and areas of focus. The sketch combines information from my public professional profile and Work IQ insights into a visually rich and easy-to-consume format.

---

## Prompt

### Original Prompt

> Create a photorealistic image in a clean cartoon whiteboard sketch style that visualises my work life.
>
> Include:
>
> - What I do
> - Who I work with
> - My role and responsibilities
> - My values and leadership principles
> - What is most important to me professionally
>
> Use my attached headshot as the basis for placing me at the center of the visualization.
>
> Ground the content using:
>
> - Work IQ
> - My public LinkedIn profile
>
> The graphic should be rich in information and visually engaging.
>
> For theearance
> - Use generic person icons where no public image is available
> - Use actual profile pictures only when publicly available and appropriate.

---

## Generated Outcome

The generated image provides a visual summary of:

- Professional role and responsibilities
- Key stakeholders and collaboration network
- Leadership and management activities
- Project and delivery management focus areas
- Personal values and guiding principles
- Career highlights and areas of expertise
